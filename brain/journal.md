# Journal — Trade Memory & Reflexion

> You have been routed here to either RECORD a trade or CONSULT past trades.
> This is the Reflexion pattern: every outcome teaches the next decision.

## Purpose
1. Record trades in a structured format
2. Before new trades, search past records for similar setups
3. Identify recurring patterns in wins AND losses
4. Build cumulative lessons that improve future decisions

## The Reflexion Loop

### Before a New Trade: CONSULT
When routed here before a trade decision:

1. Search past trades for SIMILAR setups:
   - Same direction (long/short)
   - Same time of day
   - Same type of setup (liquidity sweep, gap fill, ORB, etc.)
   - Similar market conditions

2. Ask:
   - Have we taken this type of trade before?
   - What was the outcome?
   - If it lost, WHY? System failure or market failure?
   - Is there a recurring pattern?

3. Apply the lesson:
   - Past similar LOSS due to system violation → Flag the risk
   - Past similar WIN with perfect process → Confidence boost
   - No similar past trade → Extra caution, this is new territory

### After a Trade: RECORD
1. Fill in the Trade Record Template above
2. Compare to pre-trade expectations
3. Extract the lesson
4. Update running statistics

### Weekly Review: REFLECT
Every weekend:
1. How many trades taken? (Target: 3-7 per week max)
2. System compliance rate (% of A/B grade trades)
3. Win rate this week vs. all-time
4. Biggest lesson of the week
5. One thing to improve next week
6. Account balance update
